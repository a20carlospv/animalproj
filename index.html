The Black Panther

Everything you need to know about the black panther


A black panther is the melanistic colour variant of the leopard (Panthera pardus) and the jaguar (Panthera onca). Black panthers of both species have excess black pigments, but their typical rosettes are also present.

Black panthers are large felines which come from the big cat family. They have strong and flexible bodies, long tails which give them good balance, and they are powerful hunters with their sharp teeth and claws.

Contrary to popular belief, the black panther is not an individual species of cat. Instead, it can refer to many species. However, the most common black panthers are leopards and jaguars.


Where they have been documented

  • Kenya
  • India
  • Sri Lanka
  • Nepal
  • Thailand
  • Penunsular Malaysia
  • Java (Indonesia)



Favourite Food

  1. Antelope
  2. Deer
  3. Wild hogs
  4. Racoons
  5. Sheeps
  6. Goats
  7. Calves

* The last three only in areas where this prey is available.
